# Research Supervisor AI

A **local** AI research assistant that acts as your experienced university
professor and FYP supervisor for **Quantum Key Distribution** research.

> **Not a chatbot.** A structured academic mentor with persistent memory,
> document understanding (RAG), and multi-LLM support.

## Features
- Research Supervision
- Persistent Memory
- Document Understanding 
- RAG Pipeline 
- Multi-LLM
- Session Management 
- Paper Writing Support
